Paper Format Setup

Essential Settings

  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt
  • Spacing: Double-space everything (no extra spaces)
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides
  • Header: Last name + page number (top right)

First Page Information (Top Left Corner)

Your Name
Professor's Name
Course Name and Section
Date (Day Month Year)
Date Format Examples:
Correct: 15 October 2024
Incorrect: October 15, 2024
Pro Tip:
Set up an MLA template in Word with these settings and save it for future use!

In-Text Citations

Basic Formula: (Author Page#)
Situation Format Example
Basic citation (Author Page#) (Smith 45)
Author mentioned in sentence (Page#) Smith argues that "..." (45)
Website/No page numbers (Author) (Johnson)
No author ("Shortened Title") ("Climate Report")
Multiple authors (Author1 and Author2 Page#) (Smith and Jones 67)
Remember:
If you mention the author's name in your sentence, only include the page number in parentheses.

Works Cited Format

Author. "Title." Container, Date, Location.

Works Cited Page Setup

  • Title: "Works Cited" (centered, no bold/italics)
  • Location: Last page of paper
  • Spacing: Double-spaced
  • Indent: Hanging indent for each entry
  • Order: Alphabetical by author's last name

Common Source Types

Book:
Morrison, Toni. Beloved. Vintage Books, 1987.
Website Article:
Johnson, Maria. "Climate Change Solutions." Environmental News, 15 Oct. 2024, www.envnews.com/solutions.
Database Article:
Smith, John. "Student Success Strategies." Journal of Education, vol. 25, no. 3, 2024, pp. 45-67. Academic Search Complete.
Newspaper Article:
Brown, Sarah. "Local Campus Changes." The Herald, 12 Sept. 2024, pp. A1+.

ICE Method for Quotes

Use this three-step method to integrate quotes effectively:

I = Introduce
Who said it? What's their credibility? When/where?
C = Cite
The actual quote or paraphrase with proper MLA citation
E = Explain
Why does this matter? How does it support your argument?
Complete ICE Example:

Introduce: Leading education researcher Dr. Smith conducted a comprehensive study on student success strategies.

Cite: She found that "students who start research early score 23% higher on final papers" (Smith 78).

Explain: This demonstrates that time management directly impacts academic performance, supporting the need for early research planning.

Common MLA Mistakes to Avoid

Wrong Date Format
Incorrect: October 15, 2024
Correct: 15 October 2024
Missing Hanging Indent
All Works Cited entries after the first line should be indented 0.5 inches
Inconsistent Spacing
Everything should be double-spaced - no extra spaces anywhere
Wrong Citation Placement
Citations go BEFORE the period: "Quote here" (Smith 45).
Mismatched Citations
Every in-text citation must have a matching Works Cited entry
Quick Fix:
Always double-check your citations against official MLA guidelines or ask a librarian for help!

Success Strategies

Before You Write:

  • Set up MLA template in Word
  • Keep source information organized
  • Check instructor's specific requirements

While You Write:

  • Add citations immediately - don't wait!
  • Use the ICE method for quotes
  • Keep a running Works Cited list

Before You Submit:

  • Double-check date format
  • Verify hanging indents in Works Cited
  • Match all in-text citations to Works Cited entries
  • Review spacing and formatting
